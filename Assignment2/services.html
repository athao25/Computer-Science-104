Our Services

Here are all the different things we can do to make your yard look great! We have been doing landscaping for 8 years and we love helping people have beautiful outdoor spaces.

Garden Services

Custom Garden Design

We will help you plan the perfect garden for your yard. We pick plants that will grow well and look beautiful together. We think about what colors you like and how much work you want to do.

Planting Flowers and Plants

We plant flowers, bushes, and other plants in your garden. We know which plants grow best in our area and which ones will make your yard look amazing all year long.

Tree and Bush Care

We plant new trees and bushes. We also trim them to keep them healthy and looking good. We know how to take care of all different kinds of trees and shrubs.

Lawn Services

Grass Cutting

We cut your grass every week or whenever you need it. We make sure it looks neat and even. We also edge around walkways and flower beds to make everything look tidy.

Lawn Care

We feed your grass so it grows thick and green. We also get rid of weeds and make sure your lawn stays healthy. We can fix bare spots too.

Sprinkler Systems

We install sprinkler systems so your grass and plants get the right amount of water. This saves you time and makes sure your yard stays green even when it doesn't rain much.

Building and Construction

Patios and Walkways

We build stone or brick patios where you can sit outside and enjoy your yard. We also make walkways that look nice and help you get around your garden safely.

Retaining Walls

If your yard is on a hill, we can build walls to hold the dirt in place. These walls also look nice and can create different levels in your garden.

Outdoor Lighting

We put in lights so you can enjoy your yard at night too. The lights make your house look pretty and help you see where you're walking after dark.

Seasonal Cleanup

Spring Cleanup

When winter is over, we clean up your yard and get it ready for spring. We rake up old leaves, trim plants, and prepare flower beds for new plants.

Fall Cleanup

In the fall, we rake leaves and get your yard ready for winter. We also plant bulbs that will come up in the spring and make your garden colorful again.

Business and Commercial Work

We don't just work on houses! We also take care of:

  • Office buildings and their landscaping
  • Shopping centers and stores
  • Apartment complexes
  • Schools and other public buildings
  • HOA common areas
