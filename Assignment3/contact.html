Questions People Ask Us

Do you give free estimates? +

Yes! We will come look at your yard and tell you how much our work will cost for free. No hidden fees or tricks.

How far ahead should I call you? +

It's best to call us 2-4 weeks before you want us to start, especially in spring and fall when we are really busy. But call us anytime - we might be able to help sooner!

Are you licensed and insured? +

Yes! We have all the right licenses and insurance so you don't have to worry about anything if we work on your property.

What if I don't like the work you do? +

We will come back and fix it for free! We want all our customers to be happy with their yards.

How do I pay for the work? +

You can pay with cash, check, or credit card. For big projects, we can work out a payment plan so you don't have to pay everything at once.

Emergency Help

If a tree falls down or your sprinklers break and you need help right away, call our emergency number: (555) 123-4999

We can help 24 hours a day, but there might be extra charges for nights and weekends.
