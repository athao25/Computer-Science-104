Welcome to Green Garden Landscaping!

About Our Business

Hi! We are Green Garden Landscaping. We are a family business that has been making yards look amazing for 8 years. We love plants and we love making outdoor spaces beautiful for our customers.

Our team knows a lot about gardens, grass, trees, and all kinds of outdoor projects. We work with homeowners and businesses to make their outdoor areas look great and be easy to take care of.

We live right here in the community and we are proud to help our neighbors have the best looking yards in town!

What We Do

  • Design and plant beautiful gardens
  • Cut grass and take care of lawns
  • Plant trees and bushes
  • Build patios and walkways
  • Install sprinkler systems
  • Clean up yards in spring and fall
  • Add outdoor lighting
  • Use plants that don't need much water
  • Take care of business properties
  • Give advice about your yard
